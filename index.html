Introduction and hobbies

I'm Bert Cardinaels, a 25-year-old developer from Hamont-Achel.

During my education, I’ve had a healthy mix of frontend and backend development. I favor frontend as I love providing the end-user with the most pleasant user experience they can get.

More recently I’ve taken a year off to work on myself with friends, family and a professional. I now feel more ready to tackle new challenges than when I just graduated.

I hope to find a new job in frontend development that challenges me in all the right ways and which provides me with a fun environment with a great team dynamic in which I can learn a ton.

In my free time, I keep myself busy with PC hardware, gaming, cycling, weightlifting, and woodworking (latest creation) .
